Current Research

  • High-Dimensional Dynamic Pricing with Strategic Buyers
    July 2024 – Present

    We study a high-dimensional dynamic pricing problem in which buyers can strategically manipulate observed feature information without changing their true valuations. The seller observes high-dimensional covariates and must learn an optimal pricing policy under sparsity constraints and strategic feature manipulation.

    We propose a pricing strategy that achieves provable regret guarantees. Under suitable sparsity and regularity assumptions, the regret scales logarithmically with the ambient feature dimension.

    Advisors: Dr. Yang Li, Prof. Zemin Zheng
